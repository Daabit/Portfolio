Igneous Rock Classifier and Formal Description

A user-friendly tool designed to assist geologists and students in classifying igneous rocks (intrusive, effusive, and later, pyroclastic rocks) using the QAPF system. The software provides automatic mineral percentage normalization, generates detailed formal descriptions, and supports both hand specimen and thin section data. Ideal for educational and research purposes, facilitating quick and accurate rock identification.
